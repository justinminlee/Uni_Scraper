Online study

Our online qualifications give you the flexibility to study in the way that suits you. As an online UniSC student, you'll have access to our wide range of student services, including support to help you fit uni in with work and life.
Offering an alternative to traditional classrooms, our online study options keep you engaged with your instructors, classmates and learning materials using digital platforms. Gain knowledge in your field while also growing digital literacy skills which are invaluable for success in today's digital world.
