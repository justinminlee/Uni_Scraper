Postgraduate degrees

Postgraduate study can give you a competitive edge and advance your career. Whether you need a professional qualification or want to branch out in a new field, postgrad at UniSC can help you get there.
World-class research

UniSC is one of the world’s top 150 young universities, with a growing reputation for world-class research in medical and health science, education, sustainability and more.

Flexible study options

Flexible delivery of our postgraduate programs lets you tailor your studies to suit your lifestyle. Study full time or part time, with options for online and blended study modes.

Professional exchange

Connect with experienced, like-minded professionals and a talented cohort of peers, involving the latest in critical thinking and practice within your industry or discipline.
Government subsidised postgraduate programs

With more government subsidised places you can save on course fees with a Commonwealth Support Place (CSP). You can also study now and pay later with HECS-HELP available.

UniSC has a wide-range of postgraduate study options with flexible delivery so you can tailor your studies to suit your lifestyle and boost your career prospects.

Expert lecturers in your field

Study with expert staff who have global experience and connections in your field. UniSC's highly qualified teachers and researchers work with top industry guest lecturers to ensure your knowledge and skills are at the cutting edge.

Scholarships and support

UniSC offers a range of scholarships to help with study and living costs. Postgraduate students can also access a network of academic and wellbeing support services.

Increase your income

The 2021 Graduate Outcome Survey report on graduate salaries found that people with postgraduate qualifications had annual median salaries $25,000 higher on average than those with only a bachelor's degree.

Improve your employability

91% of students who completed postgraduate study are employed within four months of finishing their degree (2021 Graduate Outcomes Survey).

Advanced learning facilities

UniSC is home to some of the most advanced learning and visualisation facilities in Australia, including immersive education environments, interactive learning technology and hands-on simulation labs.
